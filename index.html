Patent Secured — Research Translated into Protected Innovation

Translating scientific exploration into protected innovation, Avkalan Labs has successfully secured a patent, marking a significant milestone in its journey from research to real-world impact. Guided by early incubation support and mentorship from Prof. Rajib Chowdhury, Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Roorkee, the team advanced its research-driven concepts into protected intellectual property and scalable technological solutions. Shark Tank India
Kreo
Deal Secured at ₹100 Crore Valuation

Founders Mr. Ishan Sukul and Mr. Himanshu Gupta (IITR |BT|09-13|Civil) secured an investment from Ritesh Agarwal on Shark Tank India at a valuation of ₹100 crore. Kreo is building an Indian-first ecosystem of gaming peripherals for gamers, creators, designers, and coders, combining community-driven product development with performance-focused hardware. The investment marks a major milestone as the startup continues to grow its presence in India’s rapidly expanding gaming and creator economy.
National Media
Bhoomicam
Featured on DD News for AI & Geospatial Technologies

Led by Prof. Siddhartha Khare, Department of Civil Engineering, Roorkee. Bhoomicam leverages satellite imagery, drone analytics, and ground intelligence to deliver Earth Intelligence solutions that support farmers and government agencies in making data-driven decisions for climate-resilient agriculture. The feature on DD News highlights how the startup bridges advanced geospatial science with practical agricultural applications, including crop monitoring, resource management, and climate-risk assessment. This recognition reflects the growing impact of indigenous AI and geospatial innovation in transforming India's agricultural sector.
DeepTech Innovation
Feynman Energy
India's First LMFP Cathode Technology

Feynman Energy showcased India’s first LMFP cathode manufacturing technology at the DeepTech Pre-Summit, Bharat Innovates 2026 (Phase 2) at IIT Bombay. Their patented nanoscale Double Carbon Coated Customised Cathode (4C) technology enables high-performance, cost-effective, and safer lithium-ion batteries for EVs, energy storage, and robotics. The technology was showcased before distinguished leaders, including Prof. Ajay Sood and Prof. Abhay Karandikar, highlighting Feynman Energy’s vision to translate advanced battery research into scalable solutions for sustainable mobility and energy storage.
